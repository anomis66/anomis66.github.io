Genealogy a record or account of the ancestry and descent of a person, family, group, etc.

Using the tools available to me for free on the web, and family contacts, I have started to develop a page or two dedicated to the ARTHUR family tree. Obviously I will start with myself and the girls and work backwards.

Progress is slow but you can view the latest version of the ARTHUR tree in HTML format.  If anyone thinks they can contribute to my efforts, or they spot an error or omission, please email genealogy@arthur12.co.uk with the subject line 'Genealogy'.
