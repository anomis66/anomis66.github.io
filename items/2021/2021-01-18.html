TROLL BRIDGE - The Motion Picture

TROLL BRIDGE is a flourish of creativity based on the characters of Terry Pratchett and Discworld. It was generously funded by 4,500 backers including myself (see the credits at 27:18) and lovingly crafted by a crew of 300 under the carely direction of Daniel Knight. 15 Years in the making, it is a true testament to the power of the crowd, and the undying, immortal words of Sir Terry and his philosophies.
